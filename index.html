Solar Tennis Ball Collector

Summer23/Fall23 Group 10

Project

This purpose of this project is to design and build a solar-powered robot capable of autonomously collect tennis balls on a tennis court and transport them. The robot will utilize solar energy to function and a collection of sensors/camera to find the tennis balls.
